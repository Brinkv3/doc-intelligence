# Master Services Agreement

**Agreement Number:** MSA-2023-0017
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2023

## Parties

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**Meridian Financial Services, Inc.** ("Client"), a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 200 Commerce Drive, Suite 400, Richmond, VA 23219

and

**Apex Consulting Group, LLC** ("Provider"), a Virginia limited liability company with principal offices at 1400 Technology Boulevard, Suite 300, McLean, VA 22102

## Term

This Agreement shall be effective as of the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial term of three (3) years, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 11. This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

## Services

Provider shall perform consulting, technology, and professional services as described in individual Statements of Work ("SOWs") executed under this Agreement. Each SOW shall reference this Agreement and shall be subject to the terms and conditions herein.

## Fees and Payment

Client shall pay Provider the fees specified in each SOW. Unless otherwise stated in a SOW, invoices are due Net 30 from the invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

## Confidentiality

Each party agrees to hold in confidence all Confidential Information received from the other party. "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to business plans, financial data, customer information, technical specifications, and proprietary methodologies. This obligation survives termination for a period of three (3) years.

## Intellectual Property

All work product created by Provider specifically for Client under a SOW ("Client Work Product") shall be owned by Client upon full payment. Provider retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property, tools, methodologies, and frameworks ("Provider IP"). To the extent Provider IP is incorporated into Client Work Product, Provider grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use such Provider IP solely in connection with the Client Work Product.

## Representations and Warranties

Provider represents and warrants that: (a) services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards; (b) Provider has the right and authority to enter into this Agreement; (c) Provider personnel assigned to Client engagements will have appropriate qualifications and experience.

## Limitation of Liability

EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY OR INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROVISIONS, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. THE TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY OF EITHER PARTY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID OR PAYABLE UNDER THE APPLICABLE SOW DURING THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM, OR $1,000,000, WHICHEVER IS LESS.

## Indemnification

Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other party from and against any third-party claims arising from: (a) the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement; (b) the indemnifying party's negligence or willful misconduct; (c) any violation of applicable law by the indemnifying party.

## Insurance

Provider shall maintain throughout the term: (a) Commercial General Liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence; (b) Professional Liability / Errors & Omissions insurance with limits of not less than $5,000,000 per claim; (c) Workers' Compensation insurance as required by law; (d) Cyber Liability insurance with limits of not less than $3,000,000.

## Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement: (a) for convenience upon ninety (90) days written notice; (b) immediately upon material breach by the other party that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice; (c) immediately if the other party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

## Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia, without regard to conflict of laws principles. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Fairfax County, Virginia.

## Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with all SOWs executed hereunder, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both parties.
